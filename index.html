About This Project

Somalia Sovereignty Watch tracks foreign agreements involving Somalia ‚Äî including defense pacts, port deals, oil licenses, fishing rights, and infrastructure projects.

Our goal is simple: Somalis deserve to know what's being agreed to in their name.

We don't oppose foreign partnerships. We believe transparency strengthens them. Without public knowledge of agreements, there can be no public accountability.

Somalia lacks institutional memory. Deals are signed, then forgotten. Officials change, promises vanish. Foreign powers negotiate in the shadows.

We're building a permanent, public record.

How We Score Transparency

Score Criteria
High Transparency Full agreement text public, parliamentary debate occurred, terms clearly disclosed, revenue sharing explained
Medium Transparency Framework published, key terms known, some public discussion, but specific clauses not disclosed
Low Transparency Only press release available, basic outline known but specifics missing, little parliamentary scrutiny
No Transparency Agreement exists but no text available, terms completely undisclosed, no public debate
