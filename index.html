About This Project

Somalia Sovereignty Watch tracks foreign agreements involving Somalia — including defense pacts, port deals, oil licenses, fishing rights, and infrastructure projects.

Our goal is simple: Somalis deserve to know what is being agreed to in their name. Transparency strengthens partnerships. Without public knowledge, there can be no accountability.

Foreign Deals Database

Live, searchable public record of foreign agreements involving Somalia.

Methodology

  • Defense and military agreements
  • Ports and maritime access
  • Oil, gas, and fishing licenses
  • Infrastructure and telecom projects
  • Diplomatic memoranda

All entries rely exclusively on verifiable public sources. We document facts and identify gaps in disclosure. We do not speculate.

Somalia Sovereignty Transparency Index

Each agreement is scored on public disclosure, parliamentary oversight, and legal clarity.

  • High – Full public disclosure
  • Medium – Partial disclosure
  • Low – Minimal disclosure
  • None – No public access
